About

I am an AI & Computational Scientist at Aganitha.ai, where I explore challenging problems in chemistry and biology while applying machine learning and deep learning.

My scientific foundation comes from my PhD in Theoretical Physical Chemistry, where I specialised in discrete-state modelling of stochastic processes and Monte Carlo simulations. This background sharpened my skills in mathematical modelling and data analysis, which now extend naturally into the AI-for-science space.

I thrive in collaborative, interdisciplinary projects, bridging the gap between scientific research and computational solutions. Beyond work, I stay grounded through yoga, explore creativity in digital art and value the balance of nutrition and well-being.
